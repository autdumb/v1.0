Policies
Introduction

These policies describe how this writing service operates and how you (the user) are allowed to utilize this service. These policies were last updated August 15, 2024 and are subject to change in the future.

Operation

The writing service offered by us is limited to HISD high school students only. A valid HISD student ID (that can be connected with the document submitted) must be provided upon submitting a request to utilize this service. If you are not an HISD student and would still like to use this service, please refer to the contact information on the homepage. Requests are handled on a first-come, first-serve basis. There is no guarantee that your request will be satisfied in a timely manner; if you do not receive any feedback after 5 business days, contact us directly. Feedback will be provided via comments on the Google Doc submitted through the form. You will not be directly notified by us when we complete your request, although you might receive email notifications from the comments that we make.

Using This Service

By using this service, you agree to the following:

I. The document submitted through the form is primarily your own work; i.e. you did not plagiarize any part of the document.

II. The text within the document is not AI-generated. While we will not verify whether this is true, the authenticity and impact of the feedback is largely lost if your writing is not "genuine."

III. Assistance for writing the document is either implicitly or explicitly permitted.

Failure to adhere to these guidelines may result in being prohibited from using this service and/or being reported for academic dishonesty.
